Rights

Understanding Your Rights as a Patient

As a patient, you have the right to receive respectful and nondiscriminatory care. You also have the right to privacy, confidentiality, and to actively participate in your own care plan.

Understanding Your Rights and Responsibilities as a Patient

As a patient, your active participation is crucial for effective care. This includes providing accurate health information and adhering to the agreed-upon care plan.

Your Role

Communicate openly about your health and follow the care instructions provided.

Respect Staff

Treat our team with kindness and respect to foster a positive care environment.
